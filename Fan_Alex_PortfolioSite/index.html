About Me

I'm born and raised in Vancouver, BC. I've lived here all my life and I'm 21 years old. I graduated from Templeton Secondary School, and went to BCIT first doing a Web Development and Media Design course before deciding to go to the Art Institute to persue my passion of video game software engineering. I've always been a more technical and logical person.

I have very many hobbies. I'm interested in large fantasy worlds. I've played DnD for a few years in High School and want to get back into it. I've also played Magic: The Gathering and CardFight!! Vanguard, trading card games where I play against my friends.
