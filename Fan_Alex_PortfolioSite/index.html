About Me

I'm born and raised in Vancouver, BC. I've lived here all my life and I'm 21 years old. I graduated from Templeton Secondary School, and went to BCIT first doing a Web Development and Media Design course before deciding to go to the Art Institute to persue my passion of video game software engineering. My time at BCIT greatly influenced my transition to the Art Institute of Vancouver. I've always been a more technical and logical person. Art is not my strong suit, although I can appreciate good art and design. Give me time however and I will attempt to try my hand at just about anything. I have many hobbies and interests and they serve to mold me and influence me into who I am today.

I have very many hobbies. I'm interested in large fantasy worlds. I've played DnD for a few years in High School and want to get back into it. I've also played Magic: The Gathering and CardFight!! Vanguard, trading card games where I play against my friends. If I'm not found playing these games, you can often find me online on League of Legends, World of Warcraft or Maplestory. I'm usually on Discord just hanging around, chatting with friends or researching ways for me to improve myself as a gamer and player. I've been playing WoW for about 8-9 years, Maplestory for about 5 (I play on and off), and League of Legends for about 4. I'm always striving in each game to improve myself. I play a Monk on WoW, an Evan on Maplestory, and I'm currently unranked on League of Legends, preferring to spend my time playing ARAM instead of ranked. I also enjoy reading into the stories of games such as The Last of Us, Uncharted, etc.

I'm not just into games, I'm also into music and food! I have a plethora of bands that I'm a big fan of, and I love trying to eat or make new kinds of foods. I've always had my own tastes for everything, but I love to learn and experience new things.
